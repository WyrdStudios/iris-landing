Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

Start with a 14-day free trial of our Thinker plan. No credit card required. Experience unlimited conversations and advanced personalization risk-free.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Cancel your subscription anytime with one click. No questions asked, no cancellation fees. Your data remains secure and exportable.

How is my data protected?

Your conversations and data are encrypted end-to-end. We never sell your data or use it for advertising. You maintain complete ownership and control.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

What devices and platforms does Iris support?

Iris is available on web and iOS, ensuring seamless access across desktop and mobile devices. Android support is coming soon.

Can I customize Iris to fit my personal style?

Absolutely. Iris is the ultimate personalized tool. Letting you customize how everything from conversational preferences to specifics about you so it never gives you information that isn't relevant to you. The longer you use Iris the more useful it is as you teach it about yourself and your unique personality.

Does Iris store or remember my preferences?

Yes. Iris privately remembers your preferences to tailor recommendations and insights, but your data remains secure, never used for advertising or shared without your consent.

How does Iris help with decision-making?

Iris provides insight-driven guidance and organizes information clearly, offering actionable suggestions, priority lists, and relevant research so you can make confident decisions.

What support is available if I have questions or issues?

Iris offers responsive support via email, and an in-app help center, ensuring fast answers to technical questions and guidance for getting the most out of your experience.

I need to change my email, how do I do that?

Email us directly at support@meplusiris.com to change your account email. This will also update the email that you use to access your Iris account, the password will stay the same unless you change it in the app.
